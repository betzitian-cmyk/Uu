Consignment + Broker Delivery

Our broker team acquires listed vehicles from external dealers or auction inventory and completes legal sale paperwork before delivery.

1. Vehicle Discovery

Buyer identifies a vehicle in our network, or submits a special-find request.

2. Auction Verification

Vehicle title status, condition reports, and sale eligibility are validated via The Auction platform.

3. Legal Transaction Processing

Purchase agreements, disclosures, taxes, and transfer documents are prepared to ensure compliant sale execution.

4. Broker Pickup + Delivery

We coordinate pickup from the source dealer or auction lot and deliver to the client with final inspection checklist.
